Misc

no one's found me
in the reflection,
baring no resemblance
to me.
not in the place
i live, could i
deduce, how i
came to choose
the things,
i did.
enshrouded in
my green want,
madness is
anything other than
my own dreams;
embolden my
resolve, to never
again deal with
any but mine;
although madness
is nothing, a thing
to sneeze at -
although difference
is something worth
sweating over -
even though sin
has nothing to do
with credence -
no matter what
stalwart meaning
comes begging.
