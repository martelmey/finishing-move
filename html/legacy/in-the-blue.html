In The Blue

Shying away from the ruckus,
to prove your rebellion righteous,
we crave the deed
The one way track can bear
our silence forever more,
our tickets never to expire
Drift in our truthful lie,
satisfy your profound dreams
Revel in our playful violence,
our testifying hatred,
our senseless abolishment
Dancing in the eyes of the malady,
snickering and sneezing beside the wounds
Painting dread in the abortive maid’s reflection,
we do not burn to the hot pillows
Are you comforted by what we have stolen,
can you loosen your grip on the fence
Your breed as passed on the light,
our mouths twitch and
we mumble under your screams
Raise question to our synonym,
let the question acknowledge
our watchful ignorance
