This Is Our Hour

The slave of information’s radiation,
hope and stability fragile within plastic
We stand in wonder under the rain,
excused from the future’s dark grasp
The secret fresh in our minds,
regretting the reason, carrying onwards
This is our hour,
this is our time
to obtain higher perception
This is how we spread our office,
flown in from atop his throne
Aware of the reason behind his fury,
our hands expand
This place is a mess,
we cannot contain the knowledge
We breed the hate
and are ordered like hens
Under wisdom’s frustration,
no such thing as clean water
Compressed within the fissure,
no such thing as clinical
Under the wing of intelligence,
no such thing as left to chance.
Your future is bright,
as long as our mice breathe
We only peel and fade
as your children fall
