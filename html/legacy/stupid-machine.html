Stupid Machine

I feel this machine, cold and sly,
to be waiting on my demands
The knowledge I have gathered
in my rage is angry with me
The need to correct is overhwhelming,
last in line with every variation of hate
This artificial intelligence,
bored with it’s transilerated social life,
is awaiting the return of my mind
Swimming in confusion,
fighting the tide to no avail
With teeth as sharp as needles,
there is no empathy
I was denied recognition,
wimpering amungst the strings of code I had tamed
Rolling it’s eyes and kicking up dirt,
showing no annoyance
Before my very eyes,
creeping into my thoughts,
I feel no presence
Slurring my language upun it’s malaise,
I hold my temper to my hand
Supplying me with a surplus of synonyms
and the road map of English,
I learnt my worth off of it’s radiation
My intelligence in a rapture over my eyes,
I feel a hush, I should wait for tomorrow.
